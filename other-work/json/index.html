Introduction

The Synchronized Media for Publications Community Group was formed to recommend the best way to synchronize media with document formats being developed by the Publishing Working Group, in order to make publications accessible to people with different types of reading requirements.

Documents

The following are the currently available draft documents:

  • Synchronized Narration
  • Incorporating Synchronized Narration into a Publication Manifest

Status

Changes in the approach described in the documents above are currently being considered by the group. When ready, revisions will be made available here.
